SPRAY BOTTELS

Spray bottles are versatile tools used for a wide range of purposes, from household cleaning to hairstyling and beyond. These simple yet indispensable containers consist of a bottle with a pump mechanism that sprays liquid contents when pressed. They come in various sizes, shapes, and materials, catering to different needs and preferences.

In the beauty industry, spray bottles are used for a variety of purposes, including applying toners, setting sprays, facial mists, and makeup removers. Their fine mist delivery provides a refreshing and evenly distributed application, enhancing the effectiveness of skincare and makeup products.
